Friday

(27/2/2019)

Saw you for the first time in two years, when I finally felt like I moved on, There you were, still as beautiful as you were on day 1.

One thing I couldn’t believe was this time you weren’t across the hallway, You were right next to me, laying your head on my shoulder, as we strolled around the town.

Prayed to the lord, for this moment to never end, You stopped me and took a few steps forward without me, I felt it all again,

It was you, leaving, again

But surprisingly this time, you turned around and asked me to close my eyes, Just to remind me of the distance when I woke up from this dream.

Here I am back to where it all started,

When I finally felt it’s all over,

You just gave me a new memory of how your touch used to feel right around my shoulder.




Strangers

(27/11/17)

Strangers. Friends. Best friends. Friends. Strangers.

My mistake : Falling for you

Your mistake : Letting me fall for you

After everything ended for the nth time, I closed the door. I made my decision, finally. Promised myself to never open it.

6 months later and here you are, starting it all over with a “hey”. It hurts to say no. Hurts to open the door. Kept myself strong enough to not open that door.

Constant battles within me. And I finally did it.

Left the door open, not for you to just come in. But to come and leave me in pieces,

Like always.
